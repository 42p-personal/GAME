# Bestiary — The 20 Monsters

> **Canon:** Every monster is a **fully intelligent, sapient creature**. They speak, reason, form
> bonds, and **choose** to compete on the **Tamer Circuit** — the ladder of licences from Wood to Tamer
> Elite. A Tamer is a *partner*, not an owner: the Tamer provides training, care, and food; the monster
> brings its own ambition. Nobody is forced into the ring.
>
> **The Intelligence stat is *arcane* intellect** — a creature's aptitude for elemental magic — **not**
> its sapience. A Warrior with INT 10 is every bit as thoughtful, willful, and clever as a Wizard with
> INT 900; it simply channels no elemental power. Never read a low INT as "dumb."

Each entry: **species · body type · natural class · element (resist/weak)**.

## Mammals — *sturdy, grounded; resist 💧 Water, weak to 💨 Air*

### Bouldram — bighorn ram · Warrior · resist Water / weak Air
On the sheer cliffs where he was born, Bouldram challenged every boulder, rockslide, and rival ram until
none would answer his charge — they'd all learned to simply get out of the way. He came down from the
mountains chasing a single thing the cliffs could no longer give him: an opponent who could take his full
**Avalanche Charge** head-on and still be standing. Proud and headstrong, he treats every tournament
match as the honest duel he's been waiting years to have.

### Aegisox — armoured ox · Tank · resist Water / weak Air
For most of her life Aegisox was the guardian of a mountain caravan road, standing immovable between
bandits and the travellers who paid in gratitude and grain. When the road was finally paved and the
caravans traded their oxen for engines, she found herself a wall with nothing to defend. She took her
endless patience and her **Ironclad** stance to the Circuit, reasoning that in the arena, at least, a
wall still has worth. Stoic and protective, she fights to shield her teammates more than to win.

### Maneleo — lion pride-leader · Captain · resist Water / weak Air
Maneleo ruled a pride until a younger, faster challenger drove him out. Tradition said he should slink
off to die in exile; Maneleo refused. He entered the Circuit to rebuild his name from nothing and, more
importantly, to gather a new pride — a team of monsters who'll stand and roar at his side. Every
**Rallying Roar** is a small act of defiance against the day he was cast out.

### Grivvel — wolverine brawler · Rogue · resist Water / weak Air
A scrappy loner out of the frostwoods, Grivvel arrived on the Circuit on a dare from a rival he's since
forgotten, and stayed because nothing else in the world makes the restlessness stop. Standing still bores
him; losing enrages him. He's sharp-tongued, quick to bleed an opponent with **Rend**, and quicker to
pick the next fight before the last one's cooled.

### Ursath — great bear · Warrior · resist Water / weak Air
Ursath slept through a long winter and woke to find his forest felled and paved. Slow to anger but utterly
immovable once roused, he wanders the Circuit for two reasons he keeps to himself: half in search of some
quiet place that might become a new home, and half for the simple, honest satisfaction of a **Titan's
Maul** landing true. The oldest fighters speak of him gently. He never rushes; he never has to.

## Avians — *swift, keen; resist ⛰️ Earth, weak to 🔥 Fire*

### Skyrend — peregrine raptor · Ranger · resist Earth / weak Fire
Skyrend measures her entire worth in the quality of her dives. She competes not for titles but to hunt the
one quarry she has never caught: the perfect, unanswerable **Death from Above**. Aloof and exacting, she
regards most opponents as practice — until one survives her strike, and then she regards them with
something close to love.

### Strixil — scholar owl · Sage · resist Earth / weak Fire
From a moonlit library-roost, Strixil studies combat the way others study old texts: patiently, endlessly.
She joined the Circuit convinced that every duel is a question and every opponent an answer, and that the
only way to truly read a fighter is to face them. Calm and quietly formidable, she'd rather out-think a
foe and fuel her team's magic with **Wellspring** than trade blows.

### Zephyri — swallow · Rogue · resist Earth / weak Fire
Zephyri could never once sit still, not as a fledgling and not now. To her the arena is simply the
grandest playground of speed ever built, and the crowd's gasp when she blurs untouched through a **Thousand
Cuts** is the only prize she cares about. Playful and impossible to pin down, she competes for the sheer
joy of never being caught.

### Corvaan — raven · Wizard · resist Earth / weak Fire
A born trickster, Corvaan spent his youth hoarding stolen trinkets until he stole the wrong thing — a
spellbook — and discovered something shinier than treasure. Now he collects reputation instead, competing
to be known as the cleverest, most theatrical caster on the Circuit. Every **Doomcaw** is delivered with a
showman's flourish and just a hint of menace.

### Larkessa — songlark · Bard · resist Earth / weak Fire
Larkessa's voice once emptied tavern purses across three counties, but taverns were never big enough for
her. She found the roar of a tournament crowd and knew she'd found her true audience at last. She competes
for the applause and to lift the teammates who sing along with her **Anthem of Glory** — warm, dramatic,
and always playing to the back row.

## Marsupials — *charismatic, agile; resist 💨 Air, weak to 💧 Water*

### Bruxaroo — boxing kangaroo · Captain · resist Air / weak Water
Bruxaroo made his name in the staged exhibition bouts of the outback fairgrounds — crowd-pleasing,
choreographed, and, he eventually admitted, hollow. He came to the Circuit hungry for the real thing:
genuine competition, genuine glory, and the honest thud of a **Knockout Combo** that wasn't rehearsed.
Boisterous and big-hearted, he's always up for one more round.

### Koalio — crooning koala · Orator · resist Air / weak Water
Slow-moving and slow-speaking, Koalio delivers his every thought in something like a lullaby. He entered
the Circuit to prove a quiet thesis: that gentleness can win. He would genuinely rather sing an opponent
into a peaceful **Dreamsong** than strike them, and the fact that this often *is* how he wins delights him
to no end. Serene, persuasive, and far more dangerous than he looks.

### Quokkade — quokka · Bard · resist Air / weak Water
The single most relentlessly cheerful creature on the Circuit, Quokkade competes for the crowds, the
friendships, and above all the party after every match — she throws the same **Festival** whether she's
won or lost. Sunny and nimble, she's beaten fighters twice her size simply because they couldn't bring
themselves to take her seriously until it was too late.

### Sylvaglide — sugar glider · Ranger · resist Air / weak Water
Tiny body, enormous ambition. Sylvaglide entered the Circuit to prove that the smallest competitor in the
bracket can still soar clean over the biggest, raining hits from above in a dizzying **Skydance**. Bold
and acrobatic, she's spent her whole career being underestimated — which is, she's found, an excellent
place to attack from.

### Tazzik — Tasmanian devil · Rogue · resist Air / weak Water
Tazzik is a whirlwind of teeth and temper with more energy than any one creature should hold. He fights
because the arena is the only place that can absorb the full snarling storm of his **Tasmanian Fury**
without someone getting hurt who didn't sign up for it. Loud, ferocious, and — though he'd never admit
it — fiercely loyal to the rare opponent who can weather his storm and grin back.

## Aquatics — *wise, arcane; resist 🔥 Fire, weak to ⛰️ Earth*

### Maelurk — octopus mage · Wizard · resist Fire / weak Earth
Maelurk surfaced from the deep for no better reason than curiosity — the surface world simply *looked
interesting*. He stayed for the Circuit, delighted by the chance to test eight-armed spellwork against
creatures utterly unlike anything in his trenches. Inquisitive and cheerfully alien, he treats each
**Abyssal Grasp** less as an attack than as an experiment he's very much enjoying.

### Nautilux — nautilus · Spellshield · resist Fire / weak Earth
Nautilux has drifted through a thousand tides and weathered every one. She enters the Circuit not to
conquer but to *endure*, taking a slow, deep pride in being the **Pearl Barrier** no storm has ever
broken. Timeless and gentle, she measures victory in the teammates still standing behind her at the final
bell.

### Serapelle — sea-turtle sage · Sage · resist Fire / weak Earth
The oldest competitor in the Circuit's records, Serapelle has watched entire leagues rise, crumble, and be
rebuilt. He returns each season not to climb — he's climbed it all — but to mentor the young monsters and
to remind an impatient Circuit that patience outlasts power. His **Ancient Tide** has turned more
hopeless matches than anyone can count. Kindly, unhurried, and universally respected.

### Voltaray — electric ray · Ranger · resist Fire / weak Earth
A restless current-rider, Voltaray craves the electric jolt of competition the way others crave calm. He
chased that feeling straight onto the Circuit, hooked on the collective gasp of a crowd the instant his
**Thunderstorm** lands. Flashy and energetic, he's a showman who genuinely believes a match you didn't
make *thrilling* was a match half-wasted.

### Corallux — coral crustacean · Spellsword · resist Fire / weak Earth
Corallux grew from a single surviving fragment of a bleached, dying reef. He fights to carry that lost
home's name back into the light — every victory a small monument to the reef that made him, every
**Reef Blade** swung in its memory. Resolute and dignified, he grieves quietly and competes fiercely, and
he never once forgets what he's fighting for.
